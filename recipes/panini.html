PANINI

Flavor packed and texture rich, enjoy this veggie panini recipe toasty warm or chilled. this Mushroom and Goat Cheese Veggie Panini is easy to make and fabulous for sharing! This recipe is vegetarian.

INGREDIENTS

  • Small loaf of focaccia, ciabatta or sourdough
  • Red onion
  • Baby bella or crimini mushrooms
  • Goat cheese
  • Roasted red bell pepper or sun dried tomatoes
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • Spinach or arugula
  • ½ teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Olive oil and balsamic vinegar
  • ¼ teaspoon paprika
  • 4 large russet potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
  • ½ cup grated Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. saute the onions, cooking them down, for about 15 minutes. Transfer to a mixing bowl.
  2. sear the mushrooms in a bit of olive oil, until golden. About 10 minutes. Add the garlic and thyme, then transfer to the bowl with the onions..
  3. to the mushroom mixture, crumble in the goat cheese. Stir. Season with salt and balsamic vinegar.
  4. slice the roasted red bell peppers and chop the spinach
  5. slice the bread horizontally. Brush each side with olive oil and drizzle with balsamic vinegar. Layer on the mushroom mixture, bell peppers and spinach.
  6. grill and press the paninis on medium heat for 3-4 minutes on each side, until golden brown and the goat cheese melts.
  7. Share vegetarian panini sandwiches warm, or at room temperature. Veggie paninis can also be enjoyed right from the fridge!
